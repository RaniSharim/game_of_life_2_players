How to play ? (click for instructions)
1. Both players take turns placing tiles on the board, or pass.
2. Once both player pass (or can't place), the game of life begins!
3. Normal game of life rules apply, except the color of new tiles is decided by the majority of a tile's neighborhood color.
4. Once one player is eradicated from the board, or the game detects a loop, both players can place again.
   starting with whomever has more placements left
5. The game ends if it stops and no player can place new tiles,
   or one player was eradicated from the board and can't place any more tiles.
